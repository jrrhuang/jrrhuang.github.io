Jerry Huang

Hi! I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University, where I'm very lucky to be advised by Professor Nicholas Boffi. My research interests are in generative modeling, particularly diffusion and flow models.

Previously, I was an undergraduate at Caltech, where I was incredibly fortunate to be mentored by Professors John Preskill, Hsin-Yuan (Robert) Huang, Adam Wierman, and Nicolas Christianson, exploring research topics in quantum computing and online algorithms.

Outside of research, I enjoy playing racket sports, including tennis and badminton.
